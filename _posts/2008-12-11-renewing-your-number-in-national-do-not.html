Coombs tags: - None modified_time: '2011-07-17T13:45:34.469-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3597351713969111277.post-3137899952574490995 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.jaraco.com/2008/12/renewing-your-number-in-national-do-not.html --- Over the past 3-4 weeks, I've started receiving regular solicitations on my cell phone. I registered this number with the national do not call registry shortly after it was launched about 5 years ago. Most people don't know that the registration expires after 5 years... so if you've registered more than 5 years ago, don't forget to renew you registration. It's quick and easy.
